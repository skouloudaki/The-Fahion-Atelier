ABOUT US

Our Story

Creating a fashion brand has always been a dream of ours. Since we were little girls watching fashion shows and playing dress up, we've fantasized about one day designing something of our own! So, to be given this amazing oppurtunity to create a brand from scratch, to watch our website grow and evolve since it was founded in 2021 has truly been, in every sense of the word, a dream come true. The Support our brand by purchasing one of our handmade, unique products and follow our journey here, at "The Fashion Atelier"! However, just as we are grateful for everything we were given we recognize it is our duty to give back. Since our founding, here at the "The Fashion Atelier" we've been committed to making a difference. We never thought that we would actually accomplish this “want” in our life but here we are.

“The Fashion Atelier” is a French, on-seasonal, ready-to-wear brand. Its created for you, by us. Born out of love for vintage and the perfect basics Avenue is made to embody the confident, chic, and laid-back person on the go.

Each piece is created with love, from design to production.

Our pieces are ethically produced and manufactured in Greece, Italy & France. We want to be the basics in your wardrobe that you can always go to. We believe in timeless designs that will work for years to come. With our pieces we want to make sure everyone feels safe and sure in their own skin. We want to celebrate diversity through our designs.
